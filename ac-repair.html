Common AC Problems We Fix

We repair all types of air conditioning issues quickly and effectively

Not Cooling

When your AC is running but not cooling properly

  • Low refrigerant
  • Dirty coils
  • Compressor issues

Water Leaks

When your AC is leaking water inside your home

  • Clogged drain line
  • Broken drain pan
  • Frozen evaporator

Strange Noises

When your AC makes unusual sounds during operation

  • Loose parts
  • Fan issues
  • Motor problems

Electrical Issues

When your AC won't turn on or keeps tripping breaker

  • Faulty wiring
  • Capacitor failure
  • Thermostat issues

Poor Airflow

When air isn't circulating properly through vents

  • Clogged filters
  • Duct obstructions
  • Blower problems

Thermostat Problems

When temperature control isn't working correctly

  • Calibration issues
  • Wiring problems
  • Battery replacement
