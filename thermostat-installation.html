Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about thermostat installation

Most standard thermostat installations take about 1-2 hours. Smart thermostat installations may take slightly longer (2-3 hours) due to the additional setup and configuration required.

While some thermostats are designed for DIY installation, we recommend professional installation to ensure proper wiring, compatibility with your HVAC system, and optimal performance. Incorrect installation can damage your equipment.

Installation costs vary depending on the thermostat type and your existing wiring. Basic installations start at 2199, while smart thermostat installations typically range from 3149-4199. Contact us for a precise quote.

We can provide the thermostat as part of our installation service, or you can purchase your own. If you provide the thermostat, we'll verify its compatibility with your system before installation.

Most smart thermostats are compatible with standard heating and cooling systems, including many older systems. During our consultation, we'll assess your system's compatibility and recommend appropriate options.
